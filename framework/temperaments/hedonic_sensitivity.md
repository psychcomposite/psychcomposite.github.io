# Hedonic Sensitivity (DSY)

People in this temperament may be more sensitive to rewards.


| Primitive       | Motivation |
| :---------------| :--------- |
| Dependent (D)   | Associated with sensitive reward center  |
| Stable (S)      | [Ability to experience reward increased with the number of \'Met\' alleles of the subject, and this differential effect of genotype was greater for events that were experienced as more pleasant.
